# Privacy Policy for B Studio Analytics

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction
B Studio Analytics ("we", "our", or "us") provides a data connector service intended solely for internal business intelligence and analytics purposes for authorized clients of B Studio / FHDATA. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data received from the TikTok API.

## 2. Data Collection
Through the TikTok API, we collect specific public metrics and authorized user data to power our analytics dashboards. The data we access includes:

* **Profile Information:** Display name, avatar image, and biography.
* **Account Statistics:** Follower count, following count, and total likes count.
* **Video Data:** Lists of public videos, including publication dates, descriptions, and engagement metrics (view counts, like counts, share counts, and comment counts).

We do **not** collect private messages, passwords, or browsing history.

## 3. Use of Data
The data collected via the TikTok API is used strictly for the following purposes:
* Generating internal performance reports and Business Intelligence (BI) dashboards.
* Analyzing organic content performance to aid in strategic decision-making.
* Tracking historical data trends within our secure Data Warehouse (BigQuery).

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **do not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Data is accessible only to:
1.  Authorized B Studio data analysts and engineers.
2.  The specific client who owns the TikTok account (via their private dashboard).

## 5. Data Retention
Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide historical analysis and reporting services. We implement security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when we access your data.
